<p><ul><li>Stable Foaming Agents</li><li>Unstable Foaming Agents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Foaming agents for gypsum boards are additives that create air-filled bubbles within the gypsum matrix, enhancing its properties. Stable foaming agents produce long-lasting bubbles, ensuring sustained performance and structural integrity in finished products. In contrast, unstable foaming agents generate temporary bubbles that can dissipate quickly, leading to a more lightweight but potentially less durable board. The choice between these foaming agents depends on the desired characteristics of the gypsum board, including weight, strength, and insulation properties.</p></p>
