<p><p>The consumer drone market encompasses three primary types: Fixed Wing Drones, Rotary Wing Drones, and Hybrid Drones. Fixed Wing Drones are designed for long-distance flights and are efficient for covering large areas but require runways for takeoff and landing. Rotary Wing Drones, commonly known as quadcopters, are versatile and can hover, making them ideal for photography and leisure. Hybrid Drones combine features of both, utilizing fixed wings for longer flights while retaining rotary capabilities for vertical takeoff and landing, enhancing flexibility and usability.</p></p>
