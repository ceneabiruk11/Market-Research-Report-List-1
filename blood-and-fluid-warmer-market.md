<p><p>The blood and fluid warmer market is primarily categorized into two types: portable and fixed. Portable warmers are designed for easy transport, allowing for rapid warming of blood and fluids at the point of care, especially in emergency settings. In contrast, fixed warmers are permanently installed in medical facilities, providing consistent warming for larger volumes of fluids and blood during procedures. Both types enhance patient safety by preventing hypothermia and ensuring optimal therapeutic outcomes during transfusions and infusions.</p></p>
