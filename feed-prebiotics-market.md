<p><p>The feed prebiotics market consists of several types, including inulin, fructooligosaccharides (FOS), and isomaltooligosaccharides (IMO). Inulin is a soluble fiber derived from plants, promoting gut health and enhancing nutrient absorption in livestock. FOS are short-chain carbohydrates that stimulate beneficial gut bacteria, improving digestion and immunity. IMO, derived from starch, offers prebiotic benefits while enhancing energy supply for animals. Together, these prebiotics play a crucial role in animal nutrition, promoting health and growth in livestock and pets.</p></p>
