<p><p>The hospital patient transfer stretcher trolley market features three main types: Manual, Hydraulic, and Electric Stretcher Trolleys. Manual stretchers require manual effort for adjustments and movement, making them cost-effective but requiring more physical exertion from staff. Hydraulic stretchers utilize a hydraulic system for height adjustments, providing easier handling while offering stability. Electric stretchers are powered, allowing for effortless positioning and maneuverability, enhancing patient comfort and reducing the physical strain on medical staff during transfers. Each type serves specific operational needs in healthcare settings.</p></p>
