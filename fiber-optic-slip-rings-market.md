<p><p>Fiber optic slip rings are designed for continuous fiber optic signal transmission while allowing rotational movement. The market can be categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **Single-channel** slip rings handle one fiber optic signal, ideal for applications requiring simple data transmission. </p><p>2. **Dual-channel** slip rings support two channels, allowing for redundancy or multiple data streams. </p><p>3. **Multi-channel** slip rings feature multiple optical paths, catering to complex systems that require extensive data transfer capabilities, commonly used in advanced industrial and aerospace applications.</p></p>
