<p><p>The aluminum heat exchanger market consists of various types designed for specific applications. Shell and tube heat exchangers feature a series of tubes that facilitate heat transfer between two fluids, suitable for high-pressure systems. Plate heat exchangers consist of multiple plates for compact and efficient heat exchange, ideal for food processing and HVAC applications. Fin type heat exchangers enhance surface area for improved heat transfer in limited spaces. Air-cooled heat exchangers use ambient air for cooling, commonly employed in power plants and industrial processes.</p></p>
