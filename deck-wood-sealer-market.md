<p><p>The deck wood sealer market is primarily divided into oil-based and water-based sealers. Oil-based wood sealers penetrate deeper into the wood, providing excellent protection against moisture and UV damage, making them ideal for high-traffic or harsh environments. They typically require longer drying times. In contrast, water-based wood sealers are easier to apply and clean up, drying quickly without strong odors, making them suitable for indoor use. Both types enhance wood longevity while offering distinct application and performance benefits.</p></p>
