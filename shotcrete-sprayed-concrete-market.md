<p><ul><li>Dry Mix</li><li>Wet Mix</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Shotcrete and sprayed concrete encompass two primary market types: dry mix and wet mix. Dry mix shotcrete involves dry cement and aggregate materials that are mixed with water at the nozzle, allowing for precise control and reduced material waste, ideal for vertical applications. Conversely, wet mix shotcrete blends cement, aggregate, and water prior to pumping, providing a cohesive mix that is often easier to apply over larger areas. Both methods are utilized in construction, tunneling, and rehabilitation projects for structural integrity and versatility.</p></p>
