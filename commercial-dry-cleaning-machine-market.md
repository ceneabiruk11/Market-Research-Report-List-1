<p><p>The commercial dry cleaning machine market is categorized into single solvent and multi-solvent dry cleaning machines. Single solvent machines use a single type of solvent, typically perchloroethylene, which effectively cleans garments but may have environmental and health considerations. Multi-solvent machines, on the other hand, can utilize various solvents, providing flexibility and allowing for the handling of different fabric types and stains. This versatility often leads to improved cleaning results and reduced environmental impact, appealing to a broader range of businesses.</p></p>
