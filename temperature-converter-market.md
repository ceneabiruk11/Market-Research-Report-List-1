<p><p>The temperature converter market is divided primarily into analog-to-digital converters (ADCs) and digital-to-analog converters (DACs). ADCs are essential for converting analog temperature signals from sensors into digital data for processing and analysis, often used in industrial automation and environmental monitoring. Conversely, DACs convert digital data back into analog signals, enabling accurate temperature control in systems like HVAC. Both types play critical roles in various applications, ensuring precise temperature management in diverse industries.</p></p>
