<p><p>PLC splitter chips are optical devices used in fiber-optic networks to split a single incoming optical signal into multiple outgoing signals. The market can be categorized into two main types: 1xN and 2xN. The 1xN splitter takes one input and distributes it to N outputs, making it ideal for applications requiring multiple connections from a single source. The 2xN splitter offers two inputs, splitting each into N outputs, allowing for more versatility in network configurations and redundancy options.</p></p>
