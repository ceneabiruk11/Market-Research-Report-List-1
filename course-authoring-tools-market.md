<p><p>Course authoring tools are categorized into cloud-based and on-premises markets. Cloud-based tools operate online, offering flexibility, easy updates, and collaborative features ideal for remote teams. Users can access content from anywhere, fostering scalability and integration with Learning Management Systems (LMS). On-premises tools, in contrast, are installed locally on a user's infrastructure, providing enhanced security and control over data. They are often favored by organizations with strict compliance needs but may require more maintenance and upfront investment.</p></p>
