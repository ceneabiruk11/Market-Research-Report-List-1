<p><p>The CT detector market is primarily divided into two types: single detector and multi-detector systems. Single detector systems use one detector array, which limits image capture speed and quality, making them suitable for basic imaging needs. In contrast, multi-detector systems feature multiple arrays that enable faster scanning, enhanced image resolution, and improved diagnostic capabilities. These are widely used in advanced medical imaging applications, allowing for more detailed assessments and better patient outcomes in clinical settings.</p></p>
