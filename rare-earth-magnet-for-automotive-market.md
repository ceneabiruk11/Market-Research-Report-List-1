<p><ul><li>Sintered Rare Earth Magnet</li><li>Bonded Rare Earth Magnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive market for rare earth magnets primarily consists of two types: sintered rare earth magnets and bonded rare earth magnets. Sintered rare earth magnets are made by compacting and sintering powdered materials, resulting in high energy density and superior magnetic performance, making them ideal for electric motors and advanced driver-assistance systems. In contrast, bonded rare earth magnets are produced by blending magnetic powders with polymers, allowing for greater design flexibility and easier shaping, which is beneficial for specialized automotive applications.</p></p>
