<p><p>The mobile logistics robot market is primarily categorized into crawler-type and tire-type robots. Crawler-type robots utilize tracks for enhanced stability and traction, making them suitable for uneven surfaces and rugged environments. They excel in outdoor settings and warehouses with challenging terrain. Tire-type robots, on the other hand, are equipped with wheels, offering greater speed and maneuverability on smooth surfaces. They are ideal for indoor logistics operations where agility and quick navigation are crucial, driving efficiency in material handling tasks.</p></p>
