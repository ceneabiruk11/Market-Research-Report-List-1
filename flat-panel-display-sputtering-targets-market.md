<p><p>The flat panel display sputtering targets market is segmented into three main types: metal targets, alloy targets, and ceramic compound targets. Metal targets, typically composed of materials like indium or aluminum, provide excellent conductivity and uniform deposition. Alloy targets combine different metals to enhance specific properties, such as increased durability or improved electrical performance. Ceramic compound targets, made from materials like zinc oxide or titanium dioxide, are used for their unique optical or electronic characteristics, essential for advanced display technologies.</p></p>
