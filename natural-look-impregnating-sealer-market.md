<p><p>The Natural Look Impregnating Sealer market comprises two main types: water-based and solvent-based sealers. Water-based sealers are environmentally friendly, offering low VOC emissions and easy cleanup with water, making them suitable for indoor applications. They penetrate surfaces effectively while allowing for breathability.
