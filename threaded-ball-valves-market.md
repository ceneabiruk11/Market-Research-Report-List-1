<p><p>Threaded ball valves are categorized into three main types based on their construction: 1-piece, 2-piece, and 3-piece. </p><p>1-piece valves have a single body for compactness and strength, ideal for low-pressure applications. </p><p>2-piece valves consist of two separate components, allowing for easier maintenance and disassembly, suited for various pressure needs. </p><p>3-piece valves are designed with three parts, enabling easy repair and modification, providing versatility for complex systems and higher flow rates. Each type offers unique benefits catering to specific industry requirements.</p></p>
