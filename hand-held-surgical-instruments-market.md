<p><p>The hand-held surgical instruments market encompasses a variety of essential tools used in surgeries to facilitate precise procedures. Forceps are utilized for grasping and manipulating tissues, while retractors help to hold back tissues and provide visibility to the surgical site. Dilators are designed to enlarge or stretch openings, ensuring better access for other instruments. Graspers securely hold organs or tissue, and scalpels are sharp blades used for incisions. Each instrument plays a crucial role in enhancing surgical efficiency and safety.</p></p>
