<p><strong>The Deionized Water System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-bed Deionized Water System</li><li>Mixed-bed Deionized Water System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The deionized water system market primarily consists of two types: two-bed deionized water systems and mixed-bed deionized water systems. Two-bed systems utilize separate containers for cation and anion exchange resins, effectively removing ionic contaminants. In contrast, mixed-bed systems combine both resins in a single vessel, providing improved efficiency and higher purity water due to their ability to optimize ion exchange. Both systems are crucial in applications such as pharmaceuticals, electronics, and laboratories, where high-quality water is essential.</p></p>
