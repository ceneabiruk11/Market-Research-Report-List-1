<p><p>The mattress spring steel wire market comprises several types, including connecting springs, one-strand steel springs, and pocketed springs. Connecting springs serve to link different spring units for enhanced support. One-strand steel springs, typically made from a single wire, provide resilience and firmness. Pocketed springs consist of individual coils housed in fabric pockets, offering targeted support and motion isolation. Each type caters to specific consumer preferences, impacting overall comfort and durability in mattress performance.</p></p>
