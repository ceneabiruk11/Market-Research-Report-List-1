<p><p>Surface protection tapes are categorized by materials such as polyethylene (PE), polyester (PET), polypropylene (PP), polyurethane (PU), and polyvinyl chloride (PVC). PE tapes offer moisture resistance and are lightweight, while PET provides high durability and temperature resistance. PP is known for its excellent adhesion and tear resistance. PU tapes are flexible and conformable, ideal for irregular surfaces. PVC tapes are versatile, combining good strength and chemical resistance, making them suitable for a variety of industrial applications.</p></p>
