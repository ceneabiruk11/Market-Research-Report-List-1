<p><strong>The Voice Recognition Biometrics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Physiological Biometrics</li><li>Behavioral Biometrics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Voice recognition biometrics encompasses two main market types: physiological and behavioral biometrics. Physiological biometrics analyze the unique physical characteristics of a person’s voice, such as pitch, tone, and frequency patterns, providing a secure method of identification. In contrast, behavioral biometrics focus on the unique patterns of how an individual produces speech, including rhythm, accent, and speech habits. Together, these technologies enhance security and authentication processes across various applications, from banking to personal devices.</p></p>
