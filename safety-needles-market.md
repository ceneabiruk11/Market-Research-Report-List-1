<p><p>The safety needles market comprises two primary types: active safety needles and passive safety needles. In contrast, passive safety needles automatically engage their safety features once usage is complete, minimizing user intervention and enhancing safety. Both types are essential in healthcare settings, aimed at reducing risks associated with needle exposure and ensuring safer practices for healthcare workers.</p></p>
