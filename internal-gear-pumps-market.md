<p><p>Internal gear pumps are positive displacement pumps that consist of two gears: an inner gear that rotates within an outer gear. These pumps are widely used for transferring low to high viscosity liquids, making them essential in various industries such as oil and gas, chemicals, food processing, and pharmaceuticals.
