<p><p>The underbody seal market comprises primarily two types: bitumen underbody seal and wax-based underbody seal. Bitumen underbody seal is a thick, asphalt-like substance known for its durability and ability to withstand harsh conditions, making it ideal for heavy-duty vehicles. On the other hand, wax-based underbody seal offers flexibility and ease of application, providing excellent protection against moisture and corrosion while being lighter. Both types serve to protect a vehicle's undercarriage from environmental damage and extend its lifespan.</p></p>
