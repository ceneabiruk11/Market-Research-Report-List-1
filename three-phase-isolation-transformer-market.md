<p><p>The Three-Phase Isolation Transformer market is broadly classified into two primary types: Dry Type and Oil-Immersed Transformer. Dry Type Three-Phase Isolation Transformers utilize air for cooling and are commonly used in indoor applications due to their safety and low maintenance needs. Conversely, Oil-Immersed Three-Phase Isolation Transformers employ insulating oil for cooling and are typically suited for outdoor applications, providing better heat dissipation and higher efficiency. Both types serve critical roles in power distribution and electrical isolation in industrial settings.</p></p>
