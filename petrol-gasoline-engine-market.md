<p><p>The petrol engine market is primarily segmented into two types: 2-stroke and 4-stroke engines. 2-stroke engines complete a power cycle in two strokes of the piston, offering higher power-to-weight ratios and simpler designs, making them popular in small applications like chainsaws and mopeds. Conversely, 4-stroke engines perform a cycle in four strokes, providing greater fuel efficiency and lower emissions. They dominate the automotive sector due to their reliability, smoother operation, and environmental compliance, catering to passenger vehicles and larger machinery.</p></p>
