<p><p>Direct genetic testing in the consumer market encompasses various types tailored for specific needs. Diagnostic screening identifies genetic disorders in symptomatic individuals, while prenatal testing assesses the fetus for genetic conditions before birth. Newborn screening detects inherited disorders shortly after birth to facilitate early intervention. Pre-implantation diagnosis evaluates embryos during IVF for genetic disorders before implantation. Relationship testing determines biological relationships, such as paternity or ancestry. Each type enhances personal health management through accessible genetic insights.</p></p>
